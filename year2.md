# Year 2 Image Prompts (Super Minds 1, Units 5–9)

71 images (original 50 + a 21-word batch-2 expansion, #51 onward, sourced
from cross-curricular pages in the same textbook). Save each generated
image into `koleksigambar/` with the EXACT filename shown (png or jpg
both fine).

Style: same as Year 1 — bright children's-textbook cartoon, simple
background, one clear subject, NO text in the image.
EXCEPTION: the seven "days of the week" images are allowed to contain
exactly one written word (the day name), because the day cannot be shown
without it. If your image generator writes the word wrongly, regenerate
or leave the word out — a wrong spelling is worse than no word.
